About Me:

I am a very math orientated person, and I love the problem solving aspect in computer science. When I entered my high school I joined a girls coding club, where I found myself to be the only member. I recruited some friends and ended up taking over the club as its president. I have helped to bring in a group of around 15 girls to meet every week. Together we learn the basics of python: creating games like madlibs and number guessing games. Recently I also started a new club where I paired a group of 6th grade girls from Scarsdale and a school in the city called Astoria. Meeting every other week, my partener and I led these girls through projects on scratch to introduce them to the world of computer science, while also forming a new community. We plan on continuing this club in the future.
